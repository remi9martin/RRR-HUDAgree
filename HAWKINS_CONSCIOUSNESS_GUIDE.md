## THE CRITICAL LINE: 200 (Courage)

> **Below 200** = SURVIVAL PARADIGM (Force-based, defensive, reactive)
> **Above 200** = REASON & INTEGRITY (Power-based, open, proactive)

### Sales Application:
- If lead is **below 200**: They're in survival mode. Fear, anger, pride are dominant. They will resist, deflect, and distrust.
- If lead is **above 200**: They can reason. Logic, benefits, and value propositions work.
- If lead is **above 400**: They're open. Trust is established. Focus on alignment, not persuasion.

## HUD Integration

### Real-Time Consciousness Meter
Instead of just "sentiment", show a **vertical gauge from 20-600** with the lead's current estimated level.

### Threshold Alert
When lead drops **below 200**, flash warning: "SURVIVAL MODE - Rebuild trust before pitching"
